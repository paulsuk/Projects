Power Source

The power source of the car provides voltage and current to a DC motor in order to propel the car. The power source being currently being developed (2016/2017) is type of solid state battery called crystal power cells. This battery type utilizes the simple galvanic action between 2 metals and a solid electrolyte in between.

Picture shows 3 crystal power cells connected in series in their proof of concept stage.

Stopping Mechanism

The stopping mechanism of the car is to be designed such that a chemical reaction is used to provide a criterion by which the programmable circuit of the car can be deactivated, thereby cutting off power to its motor. The reaction used in the mechanism is called iodine-clock reaction, if you want to know more about the reaction, watch this video.
